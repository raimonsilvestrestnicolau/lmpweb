Main Characters

Kaguya Shinomiya

Kaguya is the vice-president of the student council, coming from one of Japan's wealthiest and most influential families. Despite her cold and calculating image, she has a caring and vulnerable side that only surfaces around Shirogane. Her wit and pride make her a formidable opponent in the "war of love."

Miyuki Shirogane

Miyuki is the president of the student council, recognized for his intelligence and leadership abilities. Coming from a humble family, which contrasts with Kaguya's status, he constantly challenges himself to maintain his perfect image while hiding his feelings for Kaguya. His perseverance and dedication make him a worthy rival in this romantic battle.

Chika Fujiwara

Chika is the secretary of the student council and the cheerful heart of the group. She often acts as a "joker," unintentionally disrupting the "battles" between Kaguya and Miyuki with her eccentric and innocent personality. She is also known for her unexpected skills in all sorts of games and activities.

Yu Ishigami

Yu is the treasurer of the student council and often exhibits a reserved and pessimistic attitude. Despite his introverted nature, he is loyal to his friends and has a sharp mind. His honest and sometimes cynical outlook adds a unique dynamic to the student council, often providing a grounded perspective amid the chaos.

Miko Iino

Miko is a member of the student council with a strong sense of justice and strict moral standards. She is often seen clashing with others due to her principled views but remains steadfast in her beliefs. Despite her rigid personality, Miko has a softer side and secretly wishes to be accepted and loved by those around her, adding a layer of depth to her character.
