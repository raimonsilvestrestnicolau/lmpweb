Synopsis

Love is a war, and at Shuchiin Academy, the two most prominent members of the student council, Kaguya Shinomiya and Miyuki Shirogane, know this all too well. Both intelligent and proud, they find themselves in a constant duel, each trying to get the other to confess their romantic feelings first. The resulting comedy from this "psychological war" makes every interaction a battle of wits and cunning.
